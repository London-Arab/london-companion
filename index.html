Shopping

London is Europe’s most visited shopping city. Oxford Street alone boasts more than 300 retailers, ranging from designer outlets to department stores. You’ll find everything from fashion and homeware to toys, perfume and accessories. Nearby Regent Street and Bond Street offer luxury boutiques and flagship stores, while Westfield London in Shepherd’s Bush provides a large indoor mall experience. For high‑end shopping and exclusive brands, explore Knightsbridge’s famous department stores.

Restaurants

London’s food scene is incredibly diverse. If you’re looking for halal dining, you’ll be spoilt for choice: many London eateries offer halal menus. Whether you crave Middle Eastern grills, Indian biryanis, Lebanese mezze or modern fusion cuisine, you can find restaurants across the city that cater to halal requirements. Popular examples include Dishoom for Indian‑inspired dishes and many cafés along Edgware Road and Bayswater. You can also use specialised directories like Zabihah to search for halal restaurants by area. For non‑halal options, London offers every cuisine imaginable—from British gastropubs to Asian street food.

Historical Sites

Explore London’s rich history, from Roman beginnings to Victorian splendour. Visit historic sites such as the city’s ancient amphitheatre, the Tudor‑era Tower of London and the Georgian architecture of Buckingham Palace. You can also admire the grandeur of the Natural History Museum. Other must‑see landmarks include St Paul’s Cathedral, Westminster Abbey and Shakespeare’s Globe Theatre. Many of these sites offer guided tours and exhibits that bring the past to life.

Parks

London is home to eight Royal Parks, offering peaceful green spaces amid the bustling city. Enjoy a stroll around Hyde Park, where you can boat on the Serpentine, or relax in the tranquil gardens of Kensington Gardens and St James’s Park. Other expansive parks include Richmond Park and Greenwich Park. These parks provide playgrounds, cafes and scenic paths—perfect for families and nature lovers.

Attractions

London’s attractions are among the most iconic in the world. Uncover centuries of royal history and see the Crown Jewels at the Tower of London or tour Buckingham Palace. Enjoy panoramic views from the London Eye and explore Westminster Abbey’s magnificent architecture. The city also boasts world‑class museums such as the British Museum, Tate Modern and the Victoria and Albert Museum, many of which are free to enter. Be sure to check ahead for timed entry and ticket options.

Rooftop Experiences

For spectacular views of London’s skyline, visit rooftop venues such as the Sky Garden. As London’s highest public garden, the Sky Garden offers 360‑degree views, lush landscaped terraces and an open‑air terrace. Access is free of charge but must be booked in advance. Many rooftop bars and restaurants across the city—particularly in the City of London and South Bank—combine dining and drinks with breathtaking vistas.

Shisha Cafes

London has a range of shisha lounges where you can unwind. In Knightsbridge, 17 on Sloane offers a private heated terrace with premium shisha just moments from Harrods. Open from 6 pm to 1 am daily, this lounge is easy to reach from Kensington, Mayfair and Chelsea. Along Edgware Road and in Westminster, you’ll also find Middle Eastern cafés and lounges serving shisha alongside Arabic coffee and snacks.
